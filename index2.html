COACH MARC

I grew up in a small town in West Virginia. I played all sports growing up and excelled in basketball. I begged a family member who was an avid bodybuilder in the 90s to train me when I was 12 years old. I remember the first day I picked up any sort of weight like it was yesterday. Young, tall, and skinny, I walked into the cramped rusty garage gym with heavy metal music blasting through the speakers, chalk particles flying in the air, and huge power-lifters sitting around talking. It was very intimidating to say the least. I quickly doubted my judgment and wanted to walk backwards praying I wasn’t noticed. Not a chance. I was completely hooked after that day and haven’t looked back since. Six years ago one of my best friends tried to get me to come to a CrossFit box in West Virginia. The truth was my ego at the time wouldn’t let me walk through the door in fear of trying something new or that I wouldn’t be good. A year later I was living in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina and I found myself extremely uninterested with the same workout routine I had been doing for the past 14 years. I noticed a small CrossFit Box opening very close to where I was living. I chalked up my pride and walked through the door. From that moment I knew this was something that would change my entire belief of how the body is supposed to function during proper exercise. I have been actively involved in CrossFit since that day. It truly changed my life for the better and inspired me to get my CrossFit Level 1 certification and CrossFit Kids certification. Know that I pride myself on meeting you wherever you are at in your journey to a better quality of life because we are all striving everyday to be the best version of ourselves that we can be. I am so excited to have the ability to share my love for fitness with friends, family, and anyone who walks through the door.
